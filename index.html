Reflection

My Reflection on the Website

Building this website portfolio was very fulfilling, allowing me to showcase the skills and projects that I have developed over the last 6 weeks. I had to adapt to new technologies and enhance my skill set to ensure this portfolio was created effectively. As I went through the processes, I faced various challenges, and learned valuable lessons, overall creating an engaging and visually appealing portfolio.

Challenges

1. Content Creation and Organisation - Deciding what content to include within the website, and how to structure it proved to be quite a daunting task. I wanted to showcase a range of projects while making it easy to navigate for the user and remaining concise. To be concise, while showing your skills was quite the challenge.

2. Design - Crafting a visually appealing design was essential for me, and I struggled to come up with some ideas. Selecting the right way to display the text, colour scheme, and layout provided to be quite time-consuming.

Overcoming the Challenges

1. For content creation and organisation, I opted for a minimalist approach. This was in the form of a one-page website, that contained a grid-like portfolio of my websites. I also used an approach of planning my idea, carrying it out, then going back to see how I can improve. This helped me develop a clear understanding of my content creation, allowing me to see what the idea looked like after being implemented.

2. To tackle design and aesthetics, I looked through various website designs and trends and sought inspiration from other portfolios. After various iterations, I finally settled on a one-page layout as it enable the user to scroll through my page efficiently while covering everything that I required. Providing a visually engaging experience for the visitors.

My Successes

Building my website portfolio was a challenging yet rewarding experience. I had many successes throughout the build providing me with invaluable experience to enhance and showcase my skills. By overcoming the initial challenges, I was able to effectively build a website that showcases all my projects.
